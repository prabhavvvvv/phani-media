Social Media

Social media has been prominent for the past five years, and has been a vital tool in our society. Let's explore social media and.

Social media refers to online platforms and applications that allow users to create, share, and interact with content like text, images, and videos, enabling them to connect with others within virtual communities, often by forming networks and engaging with user-generated content through features like likes, comments, and shares; essentially, it's a digital space for people to communicate and share information with friends, family, and the wider public.
