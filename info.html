Social Media

Social media has been prominent for the past five years, and has been a vital tool in our society. Let's explore the aspects behind social media.

Social media refers to a large range of online platforms and applications that allow users to create, share, and interact with content like text, images, videos, and audio. These platforms allow individuals to connect with others in virtual communities, creating networks based on shared interests or experiences.



Users can interact with content through likes, comments, and shares, allowing communication among friends, family, and the wider public. Social media also provides tools for content discovery, such as trending topics and personalized feeds, making it easier to explore new ideas and stay updated on current events.

The interactive nature of social media has given rise to content creators ruling, from casual posts to professional work, giving individuals power.

It has created spaces for influencers, creators, and even grassroots movements to engage with large audiences.
